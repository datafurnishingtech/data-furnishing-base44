Privacy Policy

Jan 2, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Data Furnishing Technologies, Inc. (“Data Furnishing,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you access or use our website, platform, and services (collectively, the “Service”).

This includes the credit monitoring and data furnishing features enabled through integrated partnerships. This policy complies with all applicable U.S. federal and state privacy regulations, including the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA), Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA), and other consumer protection laws.


2. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and technical data through the platform:

Personal Identifiers: Name, address, email, phone number, date of birth, Social Security number.

Authentication Data: Login credentials, verification tokens, and KBA (knowledge-based authentication) details.

Financial Information: Bank account data, billing records, payment history.

Credit Monitoring Data: Data pulled from integrated credit monitoring services, including credit scores, tradeline details, alerts, and public records.

User-Submitted Data: Information submitted for credit builder loans, rent reporting, commercial tradeline activity, or other services.

Technical Information: Device ID, browser type, IP address, geolocation, and platform usage logs.

Communications: Customer service records, email exchanges, and platform interactions.


3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

Deliver and improve our services, including credit monitoring and score tracking.

Furnish tradeline and rental data to credit bureaus.

Enable credit builder programs and rent reporting features.

Display credit alerts, score changes, and bureau activity via credit monitoring dashboards.

Authenticate users securely and prevent fraud.

Maintain compliance with all applicable regulatory frameworks.

Communicate service updates, account activity, and marketing (with opt-out options).

Perform system diagnostics, performance tuning, and analytics.


4. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

Consent: Where you have granted permission (e.g., to pull a credit report).

Contractual Necessity: To fulfill service agreements (e.g., billing, platform access).

Legal Obligation: For compliance with applicable laws and audits.

Legitimate Interests: For security, optimization, and fraud prevention that do not override user rights.


5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with:

Credit Bureaus: Equifax, Experian, TransUnion, and SBFE for reporting tradelines, rent data, and other credit-related insights.

Service Providers: Partners such as BuildCredit, LLC, Cross River Bank, and third-party infrastructure providers for credit monitoring, data delivery, and integrated APIs.

Fraud Prevention Networks: For identity verification and anti-fraud.

Legal Entities: In compliance with subpoenas, court orders, or regulatory oversight.

Acquiring Entities: If involved in mergers, asset sales, or restructuring.

We do not sell your data. All sharing is conducted under strict contractual safeguards.


6. Credit Monitoring Services

Our platform includes integrated credit monitoring tools that display:

Real-time credit score tracking (single or tri-bureau)

Alerts for inquiries, new accounts, collections, and score changes

Detailed breakdowns of credit report factors

Public record monitoring (e.g., bankruptcies or judgments)

Score simulator and debt analysis features

These services are delivered via authorized API partners under FCRA-compliant frameworks. Users must consent before activating credit monitoring tools.


7. Data Retention

We retain data for:

The duration of your account and service use

As required for legal, contractual, or audit purposes

For ongoing fraud detection or dispute resolution

Upon termination, data may be retained for a limited statutory period.


8. Data Security

We use advanced encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, and access controls to protect data. However, no system is entirely immune to threats. You are responsible for securing your credentials and device access.


9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access and review your personal data

Correct or update information

Request data deletion (subject to legal exceptions)

Object to certain uses or restrict processing

Export your data (data portability)

Withdraw consent at any time


10. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and tracking tools to:

Track user behavior and platform usage

Enable session persistence and analytics

Serve content efficiently

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Some functionality may be affected.


11. Third-Party Services

We link to or embed third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before engaging with external services.


12. Children’s Privacy

Our platform is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.


13. International Users

If you access the platform outside the U.S., you agree that your data may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. under its applicable laws.


14. Policy Changes

We may revise this Privacy Policy. Material updates will be posted or emailed. Continued use implies acceptance.
